🌙 Evening — pick one

Option A (match-night energy): parrilla de barrio near Palermo. Argentines eat 9-10 PM, so it fits perfectly after the game. This is the social core of an Argentine night.

Option B (Dad's tango wish): a tango dinner-show. Book ahead. Classic venues from his list:

  • Señor Tango · Viejo Almacén · La Ventana
  • Madero Tango / Tango Porteño (near Puerto Madero + Obelisco)
  • "Astor Piazzolla" = Piazzolla Tango, a show venue too

If Colombia wins you'll want the streets, not a seated show — consider saving tango for another night.

Cut from the plan (and why)

La Boca / Caminito / Bombonera — opposite direction (far south). Only if you go first thing tomorrow; taxi door-to-door, don't wander past Caminito.

Temaikén — a zoo ~50 km outside the city, a full day itself.

Puerto Madero / Puente de la Mujer — skippable, but an easy flat add near the center if you have energy mid-day.
